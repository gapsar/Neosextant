# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 24, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Neosextant app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Gapsar (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as an Open Source application. This service is provided "AS IS".

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is a specialized tool for celestial navigation that processes data locally on your device.

### Camera Access (`android.permission.CAMERA`)
The Application requires access to your device's Camera. 
- **Purpose:** The camera is used strictly to capture images of celestial bodies and the horizon for navigation calculations.
- **Processing:** All image processing occurs **locally** on your device. The Application **does not** transmit, upload, or share your photos or camera data to any external servers or third parties.

### Internet Access (`android.permission.INTERNET`)
The Application requests Internet access to function properly (e.g., to fetch required celestial data or maps). No personally identifiable information or telemetry is transmitted during these requests.

## 2. Data Retention and Security

The Service Provider does not collect, harvest, or store any personal data. All celestial calculations and history are saved strictly locally on your device's internal storage. If you uninstall the Application from your device, this local data is deleted.

## 3. Third-Party Access

Because the Application operates locally and does not collect personal data, it does not share any user data with third-party tracking services, advertisers, or analytics providers.

## 4. Children's Privacy

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect, or process, personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Service Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
